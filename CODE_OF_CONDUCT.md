# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, constructive, and inclusive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Give feedback on content quality, not on personal attributes.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Share sources and reasoning when proposing changes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate plagiarism or misrepresentation of authorship.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated low-effort disruptive contributions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or reject contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in a permanent ban from participation.

## Reporting

If you observe unacceptable behavior, open a private maintainer contact request in an issue with minimal sensitive details. Maintainers will review promptly and act proportionally.
